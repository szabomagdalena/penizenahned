Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 24, 2026

At Peníze Hned s.r.o., we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and safeguarding of your data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal identification information (such as your name, email address, phone number, and residential address) and financial data when you voluntarily submit it through our contact forms, applications, or during direct communication with our representatives.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is exclusively used to:

  • Evaluate your eligibility for our financial products.
  • Communicate with you regarding your inquiries or loan status.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations, including anti-money laundering (AML) directives.
  • Improve our website functionality and user experience.

3. Data Protection and Sharing

We implement strict security protocols to prevent unauthorized access to your data. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data may only be shared with verified financial partners or legal authorities when strictly required by law.

4. Your GDPR Rights

Under the GDPR, you retain the right to access, correct, restrict, or request the deletion of your personal data stored in our systems. To exercise these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at szabo@penizenahned.cz.
